# Code of Conduct

CodexIAChat contributors are expected to keep discussion technical, respectful, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Discuss ideas and code on their technical merits.
- Be direct without being hostile.
- Assume maintainers need clear evidence for architecture, security, and runtime decisions.
- Respect privacy and do not publish confidential information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private credentials, private infrastructure details, or personal data.
- Repeatedly pushing unsafe implementation shortcuts after maintainers have explained the risk.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, close, hide, or remove contributions that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations may result in blocked participation.
